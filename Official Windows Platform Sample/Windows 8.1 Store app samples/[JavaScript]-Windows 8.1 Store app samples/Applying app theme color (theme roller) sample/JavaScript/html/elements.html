Button

The accent colors are applied to <button type="submit"> and <input type="submit"> elements.

Progress

The accent colors are applied to the dots of the indeterminate progress control and the fill for the determinate progress element.

Select

The accent colors are applied to the high light of the option elements within the select element.

Textboxes

The accent colors are applied to the selection of text within all input elements.

Range

The accent colors are applied to the lower fill of the input range element.

Rating

The accent colors are applied to the rating elements that represent the value of the rating control.

Toggle

The accent colors are applied to the lower fill of toggle control.

ListView

The accent colors are applied to the selection state of the ListView items. Select any item to see the selection state.

AppBar

The accent colors are applied to the AppBar and AppBarCommands. Right click or swipe in from the top or bottom to display AppBar.
